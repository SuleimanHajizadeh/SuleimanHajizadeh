# Cold Email Templates — Cambridge PhD Supervisor Outreach
### Purpose: Initial contact with Cambridge faculty for 2026/27 PhD intake
### Status: Ready to send after IELTS result (August 2026)

---

## Strategy Overview

**When to send:** After IELTS result arrives (August 2026) — before official applications open
**Why before:** Faculty receive hundreds of applications through the formal system. A pre-application email that demonstrates genuine knowledge of their work increases the chance they will look for your application.

**Target professors:** 3–5 highly relevant faculty — do NOT send mass identical emails.
**Each email must:** Reference a specific paper by the professor, link your own work to their research agenda.
